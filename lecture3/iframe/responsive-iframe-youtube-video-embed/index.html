Things to remember:

  • Videos must be muted for autoplay to work.
  • This will negatively affect your page's speed and performance. This exact demo page alone tested on PageSpeed Insights scores 43 on mobile and 95 on Desktop.
  • To loop endlessly the video must be in a playlist.
  • To put a single video in a playlist, reference the video id as the non-existant playlist id and it will just work.
  • There may be a flash of YouTube titles on load but they will vanish after a second or two of playback.
